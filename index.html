About J. Alise Wynn

J. Alise Wynn is a mental health advocate, author, and inspirational voice dedicated to spreading awareness about emotional healing and spiritual strength.

In 2012, she experienced a life-changing psychotic breakdown during a period of intense emotional duress and heartbreak caused by failed relationships in both love and friendships. What followed was a difficult journey of recovery, self-reflection, and rebuilding her life.

Through faith, perseverance, and personal growth, she turned her experience into a mission to encourage others facing mental health struggles. Her books and devotionals provide encouragement, understanding, and guidance for those navigating emotional challenges.

Today her message inspires readers to rise above hardship, strengthen their faith, and become Mentally Grounded.
